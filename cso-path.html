CSO Path

Stats and theories come alive
when people talk about them.

Created for CSOs, this path helps energy research travel beyond expert spaces — through storytelling grounded in people, place, and everyday experience.

5 Levels. Start anywhere. Stop anytime.

1
Level 1Understanding Context
Start here›

Before you can shape the conversation, you need to understand the landscape. This level grounds you in the political, social, and cultural context of the energy transition in Southeast Asia.

2
Level 2Localizing the Narrative
›

Global climate data means nothing if it doesn't connect to the street outside your window. Translate research into stories that feel local, specific, and true to people's lived experience.

3
Level 3Campaign Design
›

How do you turn a message into a movement? This level covers the building blocks of effective campaign design — from objectives and framing to channels, formats, and what actually gets people to act.

4
Level 4Building Community
›

Campaigns end. Communities don't. This level is about cultivating lasting relationships — with the people you're working for, the allies you work with, and the KOLs and institutions that can amplify your work.

5
Level 5Scaling What Works
›

You've found what resonates. Now — how do you take it further? Cross-border learning, replicable models, and how to grow impact without losing the ground-level truth that made it work.
